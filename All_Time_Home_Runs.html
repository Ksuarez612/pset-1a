Top 10 Career Home Run Leaders

What is a homerun?

According to the MLB Glossary a homerun is , "A home run occurs when a batter hits a fair ball and scores on the play without being put out or without the benefit of an error." Nothing pumps up the crowd more than a long, no doubt homerun. The following tabel will show the rank, name, and amount of homeruns for the top 10 career MLB home run leaders.
